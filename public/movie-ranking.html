Jade's Top Movies

Lord of the Rings Trilogy

LOTR author, JRR Tolkein has set the bar for modern fantasy with these books. The movie adaptations by Peter Jackson were cinematic masterpieces!

Dreamworks' Prince of Egypt

Wonderful animation and beautifully written music. This movie was ahead of its time!

Legally Blonde

Absolutely hilarious comedy about a fashionista out of Bel-Air who went to law school on a whim to get her boyfriend back. Turned into a good story about female empowerment.
